### Arrival
- By the time [[Whisper]] returned to [[City1]] it was already night. He went back to the inn he was staying at and sat down to eat. 
### Eating
- [[Whisper]] had to wait for more that thirty minutes for his food even though there were very few people in the inn.
- Two cloaked strangers, [[Suspicious Cloaked Stranger1]] and [[Suspicious Cloaked Stranger2]] , sit in a dimly lit corner.
### Attack
- The two cloaked strangers send tendrils of shadow at [[Whisper]] and he falls unconscious.
